# Leon A

## Underground Facility

### Birkin G1 Boss Strategy (Knife Method)

**Note:** This strategy is designed for console versions of Resident Evil 2 Remake (such as PS4), where the game typically runs at 30 FPS. The knife is still very effective against G1 Birkin, but it may take longer and require more attacks than PC speedrun videos suggest.

**What to bring before the fight:**

- 2 Combat Knives (one should be in good condition; the other can be nearly broken)
- 1 Flash Grenade
- 1 Magnum (for emergencies)
- 1 Full Heal item

Before entering the boss arena, take the elevator down and grab the Hand Grenade at the bottom. Then proceed into the boss area.

Equip your damaged knife first. Stay close to Birkin's right side, where the giant eye is located, and start slashing. After several swings, he should go down. Continue slashing while he is down, then throw the Hand Grenade. The explosion should knock him down a second time. Continue attacking with your knife while he is vulnerable.

When Birkin gets back up, create some distance and throw your Flash Grenade. Once he is stunned, move back to his right side and continue slashing with your knife. Keep attacking until he goes down again, and continue slashing while he is on the ground.

Eventually, Birkin may grab you. Let the grab happen and use your damaged knife as the defensive weapon to escape. Afterward, equip your fresh knife and continue sticking to his right side while attacking. Keep slashing until he is defeated.

If things do not go according to plan, use the Magnum to finish him off. Likewise, if you drop into Danger status, use your healing item.

Throughout the fight, try to circle around Birkin and stay near his right side. This is generally the safest position because many of his attacks are easier to avoid there. Whenever he is staggered or moving slowly, continue applying pressure with your knife.

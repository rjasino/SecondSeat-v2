# Leon A

## Underground Facility

### Birkin G1 Boss Strategy (Run & Gun Method)

**Birkin G1 Boss Strategy – Run and Gun Method**

This strategy works on all platforms and does not rely on the knife damage mechanics.

**What to bring:**

- Handgun and handgun ammo
- Shotgun and shotgun shells
- Healing items for safety

As the fight begins, quickly pick up the resources scattered around the arena.

The core strategy is to focus your shots on Birkin's giant eye whenever it is exposed. The eye takes significantly more damage than any other part of his body.

Try to bait Birkin into using his grab attack, as this often creates an opportunity to shoot the eye. Another good opening occurs after his overhead downward swing. When he performs this attack, take a moment to fully focus your aim by letting the reticle close before firing. This increases your chances of landing a critical hit.

Whenever Birkin is knocked down, take advantage of the opportunity by shooting the eye aggressively for additional damage.

As the fight progresses, you can occasionally shoot his head to stagger him, especially if you need to interrupt an attack or create some breathing room.

Maintain a safe distance throughout the fight. Shoot, create space, aim carefully, and shoot again. Repeat this pattern while looking for opportunities to target the eye.

Continue applying pressure and managing your distance until Birkin is defeated.

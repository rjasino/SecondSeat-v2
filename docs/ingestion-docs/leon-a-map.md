# Leon A

Below are the list of items, enemies, collectibles and files you can get in this side of the RPD.
The status of the maiden at the back of the room is a secret entrance to the **Underground Facility**.
You need to acquire three medallions and insert them into the base of the statue to reveal the secret entrance.
The three medallions needed are the **Lion Medallion**, the **Maiden Medallion** and the **Unicorn Medallion**.

## Main Hall 1st Floor

- **First Aid Spray**: On the green boxes near the shutter in the south east area of the room.
- **Typewriter**: On the reception desk.
- **Item Box**: Behind the reception desk.
- **Ink Ribbon (×2)**: On the reception desk.
- **Combat Knife**: Given to you by **Marvin Branagh** after returning from exploring the **East Hallway**.
- **Zombie**: Marvin has reverted to a zombie when you come back to the Main Hall after exploring the **Police Station B1**.
- **Marvin Branagh**: You meet him here after exploring the **East Hallway** for the first time.

## Main Hall 2nd Floor

- A door is locked here and can be unlocked using **Spade Key**.
- **Handgun Ammo (×5)**: On the seats in the south west corner.
- **Lion Medallion**: Inside the statue of the lion on the north side of the room. You need to solve the puzzle with the dials to retrieve it. The correct combination can be found in the **Officer's Notebook** file.

## Main Hall 3rd Floor

- A door is locked on the eastern side of the hall and can be unlocked from this side. This door lead to **East Storage Room 3F**.
- **Zombies**: Wandering around.
- **Green Herb**: On the floor in the south west corner of the balcony.
